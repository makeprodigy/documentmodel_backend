The Internet of Things (IoT) is revolutionizing how we interact with our environment. IoT devices are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies that enable them to collect and exchange data. Common applications include smart homes, industrial automation, and healthcare monitoring. Key challenges include security concerns, data privacy, and interoperability between different devices and platforms. The future of IoT includes AI integration, 5G connectivity, and edge computing to process data closer to its source.
